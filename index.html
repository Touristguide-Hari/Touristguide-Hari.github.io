About Me

Namaste! I'm Hari Dhakal, a certified tourist guide with over 20 years of experience showing visitors the wonders of Pokhara, Nepal. Born and raised in this beautiful region, I combine deep local knowledge with professional guiding expertise to create unforgettable experiences.

I'm fluent in English, Nepali, and Hindi, and specialize in:

  • Mountain trekking and hiking tours
  • Cultural heritage walks
  • Adventure activities
  • Customized private tours

Popular Tours

  • Annapurna Base Camp Trek - 7-12 day adventure through breathtaking landscapes
  • Pokhara City Highlights - Full day tour covering Phewa Lake, World Peace Pagoda, and more
  • Sarangkot Sunrise & Paragliding - Morning experience with panoramic Himalayan views
  • Cultural Village Tour - Half day exploration of traditional Nepalese life
  • Begnas Lake Retreat - Peaceful getaway to Pokhara's second largest lake
  • Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek - Classic 4-5 day trek with stunning mountain vistas
  • Mardi Himal Trek - Off-the-beaten-path 5-7 day adventure

All tours can be customized to your preferences and fitness level. I also arrange special packages for photography tours, yoga retreats, and family adventures.
